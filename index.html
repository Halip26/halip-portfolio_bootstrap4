About Me

Halipuddin

Welcome to my little place on the internet. I’ve been designing and building websites for a few years. People think you need degrees to “make it” as a designer or developer. You don’t!. I started with nothing. No computer science degree, No design degree. I came from a Deficiency background. I was afraid to code. I thought coding was something I would never be good at (even if I died trying :D). I only started learning to code at 23 years old, three years later when I 26 years old I graduated from university. I learned programing because I didn’t want to work in a bank when I graduated. I found a freelance job 5 months after I started learning to code. And I freelanced ever since then.
